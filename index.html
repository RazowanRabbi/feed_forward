How it works

A simple flow designed
for speed & coordination

FeedForward makes food donation easier by connecting the right people at the right time — from listing to delivery in minutes.

🍱
01

Donors list surplus food

Restaurants, households, or events can quickly add available food with quantity, location, and expiry details.

🤝
02

Receivers request what they need

NGOs, shelters, and communities can browse available donations and claim food that fits their needs.

🚴
03

Volunteers complete deliveries

Nearby volunteers can accept pickup tasks, coordinate delivery, and keep the whole process moving smoothly.

Core features

Everything important in one coordinated platform

One platform. Three roles. Endless community impact. Built for speed, trust, and real-world logistics.

⚡

Real-time food listings

Quickly publish available food so nearby receivers can see opportunities without delay.

🎯

Smart matching logic

Prioritize matches based on urgency, location, quantity, and delivery feasibility.

⏱️

Expiry-aware system

Prevent waste and reduce risk by automatically handling expiring or unavailable items.

🧭

Volunteer coordination

Assign pickup and delivery tasks to volunteers for smoother logistics and faster redistribution.

📊

Impact analytics

Track meals saved, donations completed, active users, and the overall value created.

🌱

Community-first experience

Build trust through organized workflows, clear status updates, and meaningful contribution visibility.
